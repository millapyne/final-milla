Milla Pyne

Milla Pyne, the founder of PHOTO OR IT DIDN'T HAPPEN, has always had a passion for photography. Growing up in New Zealand, surrounded by breathtaking landscapes, she developed an appreciation for the art form from a young age.

At 19, Milla decided to turn her love for photography into something more. PHOTO OR IT DIDN'T HAPPEN was born out of her desire to create a platform where photographers from around the world could showcase their work and inspire others.
